Est. 2000

Built on Passion,
Driven by Craft

With 40 years in custom and classic car restorations, owner Harrison Cole has specialized in show quality paint jobs and full classic restoration. Sierra Auto Restoration has been serving its customers since 2000.

Our philosophy is simple: To build and help others build the finest driving classics and customs of our time. Harrison has been an avid collector of these classics since his youth and has made it his passion to bring these old timers back to life!

Bring your cob web infested, rusted classic in for a complete make over. We are set up for engine rebuilds, transmission, chassis and custom fabrication. Don't feel that the car has to be a classic for us to work on it, we take in new sport cars for custom paint and any upgrade you would like!

We anxiously await the opportunity to assist you with your next project, whether it be just a few parts, or your sentimental car that has been passed down through generations.

What We Do

Our Services

We offer a wide variety of services to meet your needs. Please contact us for more details and we will steer you in the right direction to get your restoration started!

Complete Restorations

Full frame-off nut and bolt restorations. Whether you want a nice driver or a concours quality show car, we bring your classic back to its original glory.

Detailing

Show quality paint jobs and meticulous detailing to make your vehicle stand out. From full paint correction to concours-level finish work.

Maintenance

Keep your classic running smooth with ongoing maintenance. Engine rebuilds, transmission work, tune-ups, and drivetrain service.

Upgrades

Performance and aesthetic upgrades for classic and modern vehicles. Suspension, brakes, engine swaps, and custom enhancements.

Modifications

Custom fabrication and modifications to make your vision a reality. Frame swaps, chassis work, custom brackets, and one-of-a-kind builds.

Transport / Shipping

We deliver door to door! We offer door to door auto delivery and pick up within California. We have open and enclosed trailers to support the need of your car.

Free Estimates

Not sure where to start? Contact us for a free estimate. We'll walk you through the process and help you plan your restoration project.
